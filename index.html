About the Artist

Meet the Artist

Born in Haiti and now based in Canada, Carla Synélie Paultre is the co-founder and artist behind Synélie Créations.

Inspired by light, spirituality, emotion, and nature, she creates handcrafted resin artworks that transform everyday spaces into meaningful experiences.

Each piece is unique and designed to reflect beauty, energy, and personal expression through color, texture, and craftsmanship.
