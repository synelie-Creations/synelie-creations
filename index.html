About the Artist

Created by Carla Synélie Paultre

Artist Portrait

Synélie Créations is the resin art studio of Carla Synélie Paultre, where expressive color palettes meet a calm, gallery-inspired finish. Each handmade piece is designed to feel personal, collectible, and quietly luxurious.

From wall art and decorative trays to custom keepsakes, Carla's work celebrates movement, shine, and the beauty of one-of-a-kind surfaces.
